02 · Separated roles

Fight context rot

Not a formal standard — as context grows, the model gets less reliable. Same pressure behind laziness, self-preferential bias, and goal drift. Builder ≠ Evaluator: isolated context, independent review.

These conditions make automating the product outer loop possible. They do not make it cheap, transparent, or productized yet.
