What a Real Loop Needs

Properties

  • Goal-driven — clear outcome and stop conditions
  • Multi-agent — builder, evaluator, specialized roles
  • Evidence — tests, browser checks, independent review
  • Stateful — progress and decisions survive iterations
  • Bounded — permissions, budgets, escalation

Primitives already exist in pieces: /goal, /loop, /cron, agent teams.

When not to use it

  • The goal is still unclear or evolving
  • The work is creative discovery with the human in the loop
  • Success is subjective and not yet verifiable
  • Failure is too costly without a human gate

Use loops when the outcome is clear enough to delegate. Use today’s agents when discovering the goal is part of the work.
